Cancellation & Refund Policy

Five luxury wedding resorts across Jim Corbett & Bhimtal, Uttarakhand

Excel Hotels & Resorts is dedicated to crafting once-in-a-lifetime celebrations across our five private wedding resorts. The policy below outlines how we manage cancellations, refunds, and date modifications. We have designed it to be transparent, fair, and reflective of the long lead times that destination weddings require.

01.Booking Confirmation

All wedding and event bookings at Excel Hotels & Resorts are confirmed upon receipt of the agreed advance payment and a signed booking sheet. A formal confirmation, along with a detailed itinerary, will be sent to your registered email within two hours of payment receipt. Your booking date is held only after this confirmation is issued.

02.Cancellation Schedule

For all standard wedding and event bookings, the following cancellation tiers apply:

Cancellation Window Refund of Advance
90 or more days before event Full refund, less payment gateway charges
60 to 89 days before event 50% of advance refunded
30 to 59 days before event 25% of advance refunded
Less than 30 days before event No refund — full advance retained
No-show on event date No refund

All cancellation requests must be made in writing via email. Verbal cancellation requests will not be considered binding until acknowledged in writing by our wedding team.

03.Peak Season & Festive Period

For weddings during peak wedding season (typically October through March) and festive periods (December 20 – January 5, Diwali week, and long weekends), the following enhanced policy applies:

  • 60+ days before event — 50% refund of advance, less gateway charges
  • 30 to 59 days before event — 25% refund of advance
  • Less than 30 days before event — 100% retention; no refund

Excel Hotels & Resorts reserves the right to designate specific dates as peak / festive at the time of booking; this will be clearly indicated in your booking sheet.

04.Date Modification Policy

Date changes are permitted subject to availability and may attract a rate difference. Modification requests must be submitted in writing at least 60 days before the original event date. During peak and festive periods, modifications may not be possible due to high demand. A booking can be modified once at no cost; subsequent modifications may attract a date-change fee.

05.Refund Process

Eligible refunds are processed within 10 to 15 working days from the date of written cancellation acknowledgment. Refunds are credited to the original payment source. Excel Hotels & Resorts is not responsible for delays caused by banking institutions, payment gateways, or third-party processors. For international refunds, currency conversion charges and bank fees, if any, will be deducted from the refund amount.

06.Force Majeure

In the event of cancellation due to natural disasters, government-mandated lockdowns, travel restrictions, or other circumstances beyond reasonable control, Excel Hotels & Resorts will offer one of the following:

  • A date reschedule to a mutually agreed future date within 18 months, at the original tariff
  • A credit note valid for 18 months, transferable to a family member with prior written approval

Cash refunds under force majeure are evaluated on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed. We will work with you in good faith to find a solution that respects both your celebration plans and our operational obligations.

07.Service Inclusions & Exclusions

All wedding packages are confirmed against a specific inclusion sheet shared at the time of booking. Cancellation of individual services (décor, additional ceremonies, special arrangements) within the package will follow the same tiered schedule above. Items not included in your booking sheet (alcohol, photographer fees, GST, pandit fees, special entries) are not eligible for refund or exchange.

08.Right to Cancel by Excel Hotels & Resorts

In rare circumstances — including major property damage, regulatory orders, or unforeseen operational reasons — Excel Hotels & Resorts may need to cancel a confirmed booking. In such cases, we will offer either a full refund of all amounts paid or an equivalent booking at one of our other properties, at the guest's choice.
