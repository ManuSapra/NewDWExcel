Privacy Policy

Five luxury wedding resorts across Jim Corbett & Bhimtal, Uttarakhand

This Privacy Policy describes how Excel Hotels & Resorts (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website, make a booking enquiry, or stay at one of our properties. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled with the discretion our guests expect.

01.Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information, primarily during enquiries and booking processes:

  • Contact information — name, mobile number, email address, and city of residence provided during enquiry forms, callback requests, or direct correspondence.
  • Booking details — event dates, expected guest count, venue preferences, dietary requirements, and any special arrangements you request for your celebration.
  • Communication records — emails, phone call notes, and messages exchanged with our wedding specialists during planning and execution.
  • Website usage data — IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and referral source (collected via cookies and analytics tools).

02.How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Processing and confirming your booking enquiries and reservations
  • Sending booking confirmations, payment receipts, reminders, and event updates
  • Coordinating with vendors, decorators, and on-property teams to deliver your celebration
  • Responding to your questions and providing dedicated guest service
  • Improving our website, services, and overall guest experience
  • Sending occasional promotional communications about offerings and seasonal packages (you may opt out at any time)
  • Complying with legal, accounting, and regulatory obligations under Indian law

03.Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only with:

  • On-property teams — managers and staff at the specific resort hosting your event, strictly to deliver your booking.
  • Trusted service providers — partners assisting with website hosting, payment processing, and analytics, all bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal authorities — only when required by law or to protect our legal rights and the safety of our guests and staff.

04.Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our payment infrastructure follows industry-standard encryption. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to our website.

05.Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to collect anonymous usage statistics. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect certain website functionality. We use Google Analytics to understand traffic patterns — the data captured is anonymous and aggregated, and is not linked to any individual identity.

06.Your Rights

As a guest, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal and accounting retention requirements)
  • Opt out of promotional communications at any time, with a single email request
  • Lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority

07.Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Booking and payment records are typically retained for three years to comply with accounting and tax regulations under Indian law.

08.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “last updated” date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
